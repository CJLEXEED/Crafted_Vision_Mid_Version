Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my artwork?

To upload your artwork:

  1. Navigate to the "Sell Art" section
  2. Click on "Upload Artwork"
  3. Fill out the required details
  4. Add your artwork images
  5. Submit for review

How do I set a price for my artwork?

Setting your artwork price is simple:

  • Consider the size and medium of your work
  • Research similar artworks in the market
  • Factor in your experience and reputation
  • Include shipping and handling costs

Can I delete my artwork after posting it?

Yes, you can manage your artwork through your dashboard:

  1. Go to your Account Dashboard
  2. Select "My Artworks"
  3. Find the artwork you want to remove
  4. Click the delete button
